About Sur Sadhna

Located in the heart of Jaipur, Sur Sadhna Music Institute is a beacon of traditional Indian classical music education. We blend time-honored teaching methods with modern pedagogical approaches to create an immersive learning experience that nurtures both technical skill and artistic expression.

Our institute offers comprehensive training in various aspects of Indian classical music, from vocal traditions to instrumental mastery, ensuring each student develops a deep understanding and appreciation of our rich musical heritage.
